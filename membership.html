Your membership supports our seasonal Concerts for Peace and through them the peace movement. Members will receive email/post notifications of MPD Concerts for Peace and regular newsletters. You will also be able to attend and vote at MPD's AGM.


If you would like to join or renew, please print, complete and return a membership application/renewal form which you can download here.


Standard Membership £10
Concessions £5


If you do not have access to a printer, you can request the forms by emailing info.mpdconcerts@gmail.com. We will respond in 3 working days.
