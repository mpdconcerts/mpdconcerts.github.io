Membership

Your donation, which is purely optional, supports are seasonal Concerts for Peace. Members will receive email/post notifications of MPD Concerts for Peace and regular newsletters. If you would like to join, or to be kept informed of MPD events, please contact us.

Call for Funding Applications

With the proceeds of our Concerts for Peace, we have raised and donated over £65,000 to the peace movement since we were formed in 1983. MPD is affiliated to CND and The Network for Peace. We wish to support organisations, in the UK and worldwide, with active, particularly startup, projects, dedicated to the cause of peace, non-violence, and disarmament.

To apply for our ongoing grants, please submit an expression of interest of no more than 2 A4s detailing your proposal. We accept applications by email or by post to:

c/o Tony Lamb,
37 Bolton Gardens
Teddington, TW11 9AX

Organisations/Projects will be periodically shortlisted. Your application will be kept on file for 6 months. If you have not been contacted within this time and would like to be considered, please re-send us your details. If you have any queries, please don't hesitate to contact us.
