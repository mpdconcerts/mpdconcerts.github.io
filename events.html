Sunday 15 July, 2:30pm
Rosslyn Hill Unitarian Chapel, 3 Pilgrims Place, London, NW3 1NG

Summer Concert for Peace: RIYAD NICOLAS (piano)

Riyad Nicolas has established himself as a leading figure of his generation on the international performing circuit. The programme will include works by Mozart, Beethoven, and Chopin.

‘Riyad is very talented and he has a very high level of musical sensitivity.’ –Maestro Daniel Barenboim
‘Syria's leading young pianist...’ –International Piano

*Concessions: retired, unwaged, students & children under 12
All seats are unreserved.
All proceeds from Musicians for Peace & Disarmament concerts go to organisations in the peace movement.
