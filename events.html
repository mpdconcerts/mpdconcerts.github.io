About

MPD puts on a wide variety of concerts, in a variety of different venues. They have one feature in common: all the musicians taking part give their services to raise funds for the peace movement. Plans for our next concert will be published soon.

Upcoming Concerts

Friday 22nd January, 2021 at 7.30pm

Free Online Event

Music to celebrate the UN's Treaty on the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons (TPNW)

With performances recorded by MPD Patrons Wissam Boustany, Julian Jacobson (with Mariko Brown), Roger Vignoles (with James Gilchrist and Guy Elliot) and Peggy Seeger, as well as Nancy Kerr and James Fagan, Joy Lisney and the Primrose Piano Quartet.
They will be joined by guest speaker Dr. Tilman Ruff, Associate Professor at the University of Melbourne, and Co-president of International Physicians for the Prevention of Nuclear War. He was Co-founder and founding Chair of the International Campaign to Abolish Nuclear Weapons (ICAN) in Australia and internationally.
The event will be streamed as a scheduled premiere on YouTube.
