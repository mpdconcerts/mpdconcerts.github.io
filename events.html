About

MPD puts on a wide variety of concerts, in a variety of different venues. They have one feature in common: all the musicians taking part give their services to raise funds for the peace movement.

Free Online Event

Music to celebrate the UN's Treaty on the Prohibition of Nuclear Weapons (TPNW)
